AN ENTRY FOR BARA JAM 2019, GENTLE GHOST JAM, CRYPTID JAM, & DEVTOBER 2019.

(Working) Title: Ghosts of Jersey

A young married couple died in an accident, only to become ghosts that will fight against the evil Jersey Devil. And the husband learns that his spouse had a male soul all along, and they develop their feelings one and other.

Genre: Visual Novel, Interactive Fiction, horror, & LGBTQ.
